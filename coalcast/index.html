I do a monthly podcast with my boy Sweetroll. The purpose of the podcast is to give pentesters a platform to speak about things they are passionate about and for viewers and followers to "meet" the people behind some of the awesome tweets and tools we use and see everyday. We do our best to put ourselves in your shoes and ask questions that everyone may have about our guests and about the information security industry in general. We also do our best to keep this platform CORPORATE FREE and keep the content strictly focused, owned, and driven by Infosec, Infosec interests, and the Infosec community.
